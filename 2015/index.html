Aravinth Panchadcharam

Roboticist, Electronics & Software Engineer, New Media Artist, Environmentalist

Hey there! I'm Aravinth Panchadcharam. I am an Engineer who comes from Sri Lanka and based in Berlin. Since 2001 I've worked on Portfolios, Graphic Designs, Illustrations, Animations, Software and Hardware Projects. My passion for painting and craft works had started early in my childhood. During my primary schooling, I used to paint cover pages for my school magazines. As the digital technologies gained more popularity, my interest in them motivated me to expertize me myself in the field of Graphic Design, Animations, Software development, Electronic Circuits, Microcontrollers, Robotics, Ethical Hacking, Communication Technologies and Networks.
